FPV Labs — Privacy Policy (Basic)

This page summarizes how FPV Labs collects and uses information when you record and submit egocentric videos through our app.

1. Data Collection

  • We collect videos you record while completing assigned tasks.
  • We may also collect metadata (time, device type, task ID).

2. Purpose of Data Use

  • Submitted videos are used to train AI models and build memory layers to help replicate tasks for new users or robots.
  • We may use data for research, product improvement, and system optimization.

3. User Rewards

  • Users receive rewards for valid video submissions.
  • Rewards are subject to verification and are non-transferable.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Videos are stored securely in our cloud infrastructure.
  • We implement encryption and access controls to protect user data.

5. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell user videos or personal information to third parties.
  • Data may be shared with trusted partners or contractors for development or research only.

6. User Control

  • Users may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting support.
  • Opting out may limit access to rewards or certain features.

7. Retention Policy

  • We retain data only as long as necessary for training, development, or legal compliance.
  • Deleted accounts will have associated videos permanently removed within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Children’s Privacy

  • The app is not intended for users under 18 years of age.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Policy Updates

  • FPV Labs may update this policy occasionally; significant changes will be communicated via the app or email.

10. Contact

Questions or requests (data access / deletion / support): support@fpvlabs.example
